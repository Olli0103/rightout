# CCPA / CPRA Notes

Use only as operational context, not legal advice.

- Applies to eligible California residents and covered businesses.
- Relevant rights include deletion, opt-out of sale/sharing, and limits on sensitive personal information.
- Requests should disclose the minimum fields needed for verification.
- California DROP is a separate government platform for eligible residents and has its own verification flow.

OpenClaw rule: do not cite or file CCPA/CPRA requests unless the subject's eligibility is established and the operator explicitly approves the submission class.
